Beatport Native Instruments Traktor Electronic Music Content Distribution Creator Economy DJ Technology

Beatport is the world's leading electronic music distribution platform — the definitive digital storefront for electronic music, serving the global DJ community with curated content, genre-specific discovery, and high-quality audio distribution. Working inside this ecosystem gave me deep understanding of content creator ecosystems, digital distribution platforms, and the music technology stack.

My career across the music ecosystem spans the complete technology platform: content creation (Ableton, Logic, synthesis), distribution (Beatport platform, digital content delivery), performance (Traktor by Native Instruments, MIDI controllers, the Miselu C.24), visual production (generative visuals, projection mapping), and venue-scale installation design. I also managed audio and visual software integrations between Traktor and major real-time visual platforms such as Resolume and TouchDesigner — solutions adopted by top DJs in festival environments, as well as by internationally renowned artists including Radiohead and Nine Inch Nails for their live concert productions.

Music Technology Stack · Creation → Distribution → Performance

This background built a unique skill set sitting at the intersection of visual design, real-time systems, live performance, and audience experience at massive scale. The same multi-input, real-time, human-in-the-loop design thinking directly parallels the AI agent interfaces and streaming data systems I design today.

Understanding creator workflows, marketplace UX, content discovery patterns, and the economics of digital distribution — these capabilities transfer directly to platform design for any content-driven ecosystem, from app stores to streaming services to AI marketplaces.

From content creation through distribution to live performance — fluency across a complete technology platform.
